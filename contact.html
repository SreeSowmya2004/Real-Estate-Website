FAQ

how to cancel booking?

Cancel online real estate bookings by logging in, finding your booking, and checking the cancellation policy. If available, use the online cancellation option and get confirmation. For issues, contact customer support with your booking details. Keep records and monitor refunds if applicable.

when will I get the possession?

Your possession date is in your sale agreement and potentially on the TGRERA website for registered projects. Construction progress and the Occupancy Certificate also matter. Check your agreement and contact the builder for updates. Delays beyond agreed terms or RERA dates give you rights under RERA.

where can I pay the rent?

Pay rent online (apps, bank transfer, UPI) or offline (cash with receipt, cheque, money order if needed). Check your agreement and always get a receipt.

how to contact with the buyers?

Contact us by listing online (portals, social media), using a real estate agent's network, holding open houses, and putting up a "For Sale" sign. Respond professionally and be ready for viewings.

why my listing not showing up?

Listing not showing? Could be processing, wrong search, incomplete info, account problems, platform issues, pending approval, or expired. Check your account and contact support.

how to promote my listing?

Promote your Hyderabad listing online (good photos, paid boosts, social media), work with agents, use signs and open houses, highlight location benefits, and track results.
